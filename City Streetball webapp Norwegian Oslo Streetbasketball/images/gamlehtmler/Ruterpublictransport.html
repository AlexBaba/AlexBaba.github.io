Ruter in Norway is a common administration company for public transport in Oslo og Akershus when people travel with bus, tram, train and subway. Users gets navigated to the nearest stations with public transport closest possible to where you want to go
On this website Ruter.no/en (directly link to website) you will find all travel information, ticket prices and how to get there with bus or the subway.
All there is to know is to click where you shall travel from and desired location of destination place or adress user gets from the map section of the app (Trip Type Selection - Main page) in the Google Maps location finder.
